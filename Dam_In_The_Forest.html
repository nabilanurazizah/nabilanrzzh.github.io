5. Settings

It is a post-apocalyptic world.

As air pollution worsened due to resource depletion, only the wealthy migrated to space, and the rest of the people who were judged to be useless were left on Earth. After that, space migrants fired missiles into the earth to make a clean earth and live, and most of the people who survived died. Since then, many people have died from air pollution and infectious diseases.

Currently, except for isolated villages such as Nerine, Batz, and Syngonium, the earth's environment has changed so badly that it is impossible to breathe properly without a mask on the ground. It is presumed that the migrants who migrated into space were also wiped out shortly thereafter.

5.1 Region

  • Nerine
    Located in the most remote part of it's country, it is a closed village with almost no interaction with the outside world. The birthplace of Dam and Mish.
  • Bartz
    ]It is the birthplace of Flo, Rena, and Yuli. Unproductive villages that actually make garbage factory. It seems that they live by spinning, but according to Yuli, sometimes a dust storm flies through an experiment.
  • Syngonium
    It is the birthplace of Nike, and unlike Bartz, which runs a factory, it seems that they live mainly by farming. Unlike Bartz, which is real human garbage, the syngonium people seem to have the least conscience.There is a place where the villagers are very gentle and cute, such as having the person who cooks the best as the village chief.
  • Other regions
    Have not yet been identified. Those villages are isolated in one district. According to what has been revealed so far, the quality of the air outside is so bad that it is difficult to breathe even with a mask on. Also, that's why you can't see a single inch ahead.
